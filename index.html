How to Access the Canon IJ Printer Setup Page

  1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, etc.).
  2. Type https://ij.start.canon into the address bar.
  3. Press Enter to reach the Canon : Official ｜ ij.start.canon home page.
  4. Enter your printer model in the search field and press Search.
  5. Select your operating system if prompted to get the correct driver.

This is the safest and fastest way to begin installation for any Canon printer.

Downloading Canon Printer Drivers

  1. Click Download to get the driver installer for your operating system.
  2. Save the installer file to your computer.
  3. Make sure you choose the latest version to avoid bugs or security vulnerabilities.

Canon’s site will often recommend additional utilities such as scanning software, photo printing tools, and firmware updates.

Installing Your Canon Printer (Windows)

  1. Locate and open the downloaded installer file.
  2. Click Start Setup when prompted.
  3. Accept the license agreement and choose your region.
  4. Select Wireless Setup or USB Setup depending on your preference.
  5. Follow the prompts to detect your printer.
  6. Enter your Wi-Fi password if doing wireless setup.
  7. Wait for the installation to finish and run a test print.

Installing Your Canon Printer (Mac)

  1. Open the driver file you downloaded from Canon : Official ｜ ij.start.canon.
  2. Follow the on-screen prompts to begin installation.
  3. For Wi-Fi setup, select your network and enter your credentials.
  4. Add your printer manually if it does not appear by going to System Preferences → Printers & Scanners.
  5. Confirm connection by printing a sample page.

Connecting Your Canon Printer to Wi-Fi

  • Printers with Touchscreens: Navigate to Network Settings → Wireless LAN Setup, select your network, and enter the password.
  • Printers without Touchscreens: Press and hold the Wi-Fi button until the light flashes, then press the WPS button on your router.

Once connected, your printer should appear automatically during driver installation.

Using Canon Mobile Printing

  1. Download the Canon PRINT Inkjet/SELPHY app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Connect your mobile device to the same Wi-Fi network as your printer.
  3. Open the app and add your printer.

This is useful for printing documents, photos, and web pages directly from your mobile device without turning on a PC.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Printer not found: Ensure your printer and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Driver installation fails: Disable antivirus temporarily and retry.
  • Slow printing: Update your printer firmware and check your Wi-Fi speed.
  • Paper jams or ink errors: Follow your model’s manual instructions for clearing jams and replacing cartridges.
